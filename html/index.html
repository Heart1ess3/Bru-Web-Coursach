"Fall Season Recommendations"

— Regularly treat your pet with flea and tick prevention products to protect them from dangerous diseases. Also, don't forget sunscreen for short-haired and light-colored animals.

"Winter Season Recommendations"

— Do not take domestic animals on long walks in severe frost. Be sure to provide your pet with warm clothing and paw protection. Don't forget to regularly check for frostbite.

"Spring Season Recommendations"

— After the snow melts, carefully inspect your pet for ticks. Help the animal molt painlessly by providing proper nutrition and care.
